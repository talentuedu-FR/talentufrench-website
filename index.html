Course Highlights

Beginner Friendly

From syllables to basic vocabulary, we help students work on French one step at a time.

Flexibility

For students with French learning experience, we offer assessment for more advanced courses (A2-B1-B2).

Interaction

Our small classes of 4-8 can ensure each student can receive feedback from the teacher.
